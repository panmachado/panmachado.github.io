About me

I got my Physics diploma from the Universidade Federal do Ceará (2007), and the master (2009) and Ph.D. (2013) from the Universidade de São Paulo under the supervision of Prof. Renata Zukanovich Funchal. I did my first postdoc at the Universidad Autónoma de Madrid, and my second postdoc at Fermilab. I have been an associate scientist at Fermilab since 2017. I work on theoretical high energy physics, and I am particularly interested in neutrino, Higgs and dark matter physics.
